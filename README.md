# ninio-mar

Working repository for **El niño que vino del mar**.

This repository is a clean manufacturing scaffold, not the finished manuscript. Its job is to protect the book from flattening: source material stays source material, the spine stays structural, and living-person risk is handled before drafting.

## Current canonical frame

**Working title:** Do Not Graft From Memory Without Testing Life

**Public/book title branch:** El niño que vino del mar

**Form:** autobiographical repair manual in the form of family myth.

**Core sentence:** This is not the story of what happened. This is the story of what happens to families when what happened cannot be safely carried in one form.

## Repo intent

1. Preserve Drive material as source references.
2. Separate source, extraction, reduction, and manuscript.
3. Prevent forbidden symbol clusters.
4. Track placeholders without inventing missing scenes.
5. Keep canon constraints close to the drafts.
